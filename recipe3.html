Salad

Prep: 10 mins Difficulty: Easy Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • ½ cup croutons
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry all the vegetables thoroughly.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and grated carrot.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat everything evenly.
  5. Top with croutons just before serving to keep them crunchy.
  6. Serve immediately as a starter or a light main course.
