Pasta

Prep: 20 mins Difficulty: Easy Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or penne
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley to garnish

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the packet instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well. Let it simmer on low heat for 3–4 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan and stir until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce until fully coated.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan if desired.
